Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a civic issue? expand_more

You can report an issue by using our mobile app or by clicking the "Report an Issue" button on our website. Please provide specific location details and photos for a faster response.

How long does it take to resolve issues? expand_more

Resolution times vary depending on the issue type and severity. Most non-emergency issues are addressed within 5-7 business days. You can track the status using the tracking ID provided upon submission.

Is my personal information kept private? expand_more

Yes, your privacy is important. Your contact information is only used to provide updates on your report and will not be shared publicly. You can choose to report issues anonymously, though this may limit our ability to follow up.
